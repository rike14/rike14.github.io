What is the 'snake effect', which shows that the medicine can be worse than the disease - BBC News Brasil Human deaths per year: about 80,000 Greatest danger: venom sting Snakes, also called ophidians, snakes, mbois, mboias and malacatifas, are legless poikilothermic reptiles belonging to the suborder Serpentes, or Ophidia. Although there are snakes of different behaviors, more calm or more aggressive, snakes are very feared animals. Encounters with snakes can generate many accidents, some being fatal.
