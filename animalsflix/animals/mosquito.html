Mosquito 1#

Against dengue: company genetically modifies mosquitoes and will release them in the US - Canaltech Human deaths per year: about 1 million Biggest danger: bite transmits diseases Although seemingly small and harmless, mosquitoes pose a great danger to humans. As it is difficult to avoid a mosquito bite, they are a potential risk to people's health. Some of the diseases transmitted by the bite are: dengue, zika virus, yellow fever, malaria and chikungunya.
